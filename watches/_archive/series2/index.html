Series 2

Designed and made on the Isle of Man

“

“My new Series 2 is an evolution of the model which has been perfected over the last ten years since its original release.”

“Roger’s new Series 2 is a 38/40mm cased, manually wound wristwatch displaying hours, minutes, seconds and power reserve dial.”

Series 2

Featuring - hours, minutes, seconds and up down indicator

Movement:

  • Roger W Smith’s latest single wheel version of the Daniels Co-axial escapement
  • Free sprung Quadrajust balance
  • 18,000 vibrations per hour
  • Raised barrel bridge and balance cock, engraved with a choice of English floral designs
  • English finished with frosted and gilded plates
  • Gold chatons and flame coloured screws to a purple/blue hue
  • Numbered and signed, R.W. Smith - Isle of Man

Dial & Hands:

  • A choice of materials can be used to create a unique engraved or engine turned dial and a complementary set of the unique Roger W Smith’s sculpted hands

Case:

  • A choice of platinum or 18 carat yellow, red and white golds
  • A choice of 38mm or 40mm diameter
  • Sapphire crystals to front and rear

Strap & Buckle:

  • A leather strap with matching buckle to suit the case material
  • Engraved R.W. Smith buckle

Presentation Box:

  • An English worked, leather presentation box

Series 2

Roger’s notes

“This is probably the most important watch in the history of the company. The original Series 2 was the first watch to be completely made in-house (2006). It was a huge step forward for us from the Series 1 and was also the first production watch by a British watchmaker in modern times.


From a personal perspective the Series 2 was developed during the most challenging period in the history of the business; its genesis therefore didn’t only result in a watch - it re-invented the studio and assured our future.


Our evolved Series 2 represents the culmination of everything we have learned over the last 15 years. While the new second generation single-wheel Co-axial escapement is the driving force behind all our watches, the spirit of that original Series 2 is their heart!”
